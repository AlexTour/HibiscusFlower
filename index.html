Hibiscus for Sudan

Honoring the Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis), a symbol of resilience in Sudan. Join the movement to support civilians affected by the ongoing conflict.

Why We Raise Our Voices

Sudan continues to face one of the world’s most devastating civil conflicts. Millions of people have been displaced, families separated, and essential resources cut off. Our movement focuses on raising international awareness, amplifying Sudanese voices, and supporting grassroots humanitarian efforts.

The hibiscus flower represents beauty and resilience — a symbol of Sudan’s strength. By engaging with this project, you help spread the story of a nation that deserves peace.
